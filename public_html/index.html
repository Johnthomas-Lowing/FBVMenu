Our ultra-clean & professional vapor lounges are located throughout Alaska, Oregon, and Washington, with future growth planned nationwide . Our ambition from the beginning was to create a comfortable lounge environment where smokers and vapers will feel welcome to learn more about electronic cigarettes- whether you are 19 or 90. We are ready to answer your questions and arm you with what you need to cut the combustion, smoke and tar out of your life.

Each of our stores feature over 100 flavors of eLiquid (also called eJuice) from professional, boutique US manufacturers - all available in various nicotine strengths & all available for sampling.

We stock everything from top-quality electronic cigarette starter kits all the way up to authentic mechanical mods and variable boxes - with the knowledge you need to operate them! We constantly vary our collection of new products so keep checking back for more.

For your comfort, each of our stores feature commercial air handling systems that incorporate HEPA & carbon filtration to provide you with the cleanest air possible.
